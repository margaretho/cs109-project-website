Background and Motivation

Being food enthusiasts with an interest in business, analyzing Yelp's data was a natural choice for our final project. Many people use Yelp to discover new local restaurants and to share their opinions about their experiences. Broadly, we hoped to learn more about the Yelp user base and discover trends in the ratings and reviews, which could be useful if any of us ever decided to start a restaurant. With the large amount of data available through the Yelp Dataset Challenge, we were able to uncover a few key insights about the businesses in Phoenix, AZ.

Do elite users rate differently from normal users?

We found the elite users' average ratings are normally distributed, but many normal users have an average rating of 5 stars. We suspected that this was due to many normal users only giving a single 5 star review.

Additionally, elite users' reviews are much longer for each star rating, whereas normal users' 5 star reviews are very short. This information and the above finding led us to believe that there is a considerable number of reviews from fake accounts on Yelp.



Are there a lot of users who give single 5-star reviews?

Users who have only written a single review tend to either give 5 star or 1 star reviews.
